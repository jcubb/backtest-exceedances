Metric definitions
Parametric
Equal-weight standard deviation, scaled to VaR with the 99% Normal z-score.
Historical
Empirical worst 1% of returns over the window (equal-weight historical simulation).
Expected Shortfall (ES)
Mean of the worst 2.55% of returns (97.45% expected shortfall = 99% Normal VaR).
EWMA
One-year half-life weighted standard deviation, scaled by the 99% Normal z-score.
BRW
Boudoukh–Richardson–Whitelaw: half-life weighted worst 1% quantile (weighted historical simulation).
BRW-ES
Half-life weighted mean of the worst 2.55% of returns (weighted expected shortfall).
Dotted line = current methodology (hist_3y) when present. VaR shown as a positive loss (e.g. 2.33%); exceedance count = days in the trailing year whose realised loss exceeded the VaR.
